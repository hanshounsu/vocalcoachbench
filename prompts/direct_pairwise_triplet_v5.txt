Compare two singing audios as a vocal coach.

You will receive two labeled audio inputs: Audio A and Audio B.
Audio A and Audio B are different performances of the same singing task. Choose which performance is better overall for vocal quality and musical execution.

Return one valid JSON object only. Start with { and end with }. Quote all keys and strings. Do not use markdown or YAML.

Required keys:
- winner: "A" or "B"
- confidence: one number from 0.0 to 1.0
- rationale: one short audio-grounded reason for the choice

Judge the relative performance quality. If the performances are close, still choose the slightly better one.
